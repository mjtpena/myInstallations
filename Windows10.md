
# Manual Installations 


##[Chocolatey](https://chocolatey.org/install)
* Open Powershell as Administraor
* Get-ExecutionPolicy (Not Restricted)
* iwr https://chocolatey.org/install.ps1 -UseBasicParsing | iex
